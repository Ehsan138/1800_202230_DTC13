Categorizing your habits.


Click on each tab below to learn more about how we define habits.

Overconsumption is using something excessively more than you need. This could involve buying too many products, eating too much food, or watching too much television. We have all probably heard the phrase “everything in moderation,” but we often struggle to follow that guide, and this has led to a cycle of unhealthy habits.

The way we define underconsumption is if you are lacking something. For example, calorie underconsumption generally has negative effects on your body through physical performance, and host defense against infection. These effects can be mitigated incorporating more protein, mineral, and vitamin intakes into your diet.
